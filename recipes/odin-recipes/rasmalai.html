Rasmalai Recipe

How to make Rasmalai

Rasmalai is a popular Indian dessert made with milk, sugar, and saffron. This recipe will show you how to make soft and spongy rasmalai at home. Rasmalai is a delicious dessert that is perfect for special occasions or festivals.

Ingredients

  • Milk: Whole milk is used to make the chenna for the rasmalai.
  • Lemon Juice: Lemon juice is used to curdle the milk and make chenna.
  • Sugar: Sugar is used to sweeten the milk and the chenna balls.
  • Saffron: Saffron is used to flavor the milk and give it a beautiful yellow color.
  • Cardamom: Cardamom is used to flavor the milk and the chenna balls.
  • Pistachios and Almonds: Pistachios and almonds are used to garnish the rasmalai.

Steps

  1. Boil the milk.
  2. Add lemon juice to curdle the milk.
  3. Strain the chenna and rinse it with water.
  4. Knead the chenna until smooth.
  5. Shape the chenna into small balls.
  6. Boil the chenna balls in sugar syrup.
  7. Soak the chenna balls in sweetened milk.
  8. Garnish with pistachios and almonds.
