Lasagna Recipe

How to make lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made of stacked layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese. This recipe will show you how to make a classic lasagna with layers of ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat: This is super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoon of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasonings, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles: Use store-brought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheese: Parmesan, Mozzarella, and ricotta cheese makes this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta cheese so it doesn't ooze of the lasagna when you cut into it.

    • Steps

    1. Make the meat sauce.
    2. Cook the noodles.
    3. Make the ricotta mixture.
    4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
    5. Cover the foil and bake.
    6. Let the lasagna rest before servings.
