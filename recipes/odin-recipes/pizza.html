Pizza Recipe

How to make Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a flat, round base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. This recipe will show you how to make a delicious pizza at home. Pizza is a versatile dish that can be customized with your favorite ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Dough: Flour, water, yeast, salt, and sugar are used to make the pizza dough.
  • Sauce: Tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and herbs are used to make the pizza sauce.
  • Cheese: Mozzarella cheese is commonly used to top the pizza.
  • Toppings: Pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, and olives are popular pizza toppings.
  • Herbs: Basil, oregano, and red pepper flakes are used to season the pizza.

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough and let it rise.
  2. Roll out the dough into a round shape.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce on the dough.
  4. Sprinkle cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake the pizza in a preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  7. Sprinkle herbs over the pizza before serving.
